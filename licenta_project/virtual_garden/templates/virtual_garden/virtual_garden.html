General Plant Care Tips
  • Watering: Check soil moisture before watering. Most plants prefer slightly moist soil.
  • Light: Provide adequate light based on plant requirements. Rotate plants weekly.
  • Fertilizing: Feed during growing season (spring/summer) with balanced fertilizer.
  • Pruning: Remove dead or diseased parts regularly to promote healthy growth.
  • Repotting: Repot when roots outgrow the container, usually every 1-2 years.
